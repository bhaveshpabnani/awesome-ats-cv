# Action Verbs

Use precise verbs and avoid repeating the same opener. Choose verbs that match the actual work.

## Engineering and systems

- Architected
- Built
- Designed
- Developed
- Engineered
- Implemented
- Integrated
- Optimized
- Refactored
- Shipped
- Scaled
- Automated
- Deployed
- Instrumented
- Hardened
- Debugged
- Migrated
- Modularized
- Streamlined
- Validated

- Analyzed

## Weak openers to replace

- Helped
- Worked on
- Responsible for
- Participated in
- Contributed to
- Involved in
- Used
- Made
- Did
- Learned
- Assisted

## Verb de-duplication rule

In the same role or project, avoid starting two bullets with the same verb. Across the full CV, repeated verbs are acceptable only when they are the best precise word and not visually monotonous.
